Set back from the street, open to the north.

A long, low form drawn back from the kerb to make room for a deep northern garden. The plan is pulled apart in the middle so light reaches the living edge from two sides — morning sun in the kitchen, late afternoon over the lounge.

Quiet to live in, easy to keep, and built to hold its value as the estate matures around it.
